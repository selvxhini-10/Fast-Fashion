Did you know?

  • It's estimated that the fashion industry will contribute to 25% of the world's carbon emissions by 2050

  • 92 million tonnes of textiles waste end up in landfills of the 100 billion that are produced each year. This means a garbage truck packed full of clothes is disposed of in landfills each second.

  • Fast Fashion produces half a million tons of microplastics

  • 1 in 6 people in the world works a job related to the fashion. The industry employs people worldwide, including "some of the most highest and lowest paid individuals".
